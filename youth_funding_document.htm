APPLICATION FOR FUNDING.

South Cerney Youth Parish Council supports the provision of activities/ projects for young people in the South Cerney area of Gloucestershire. As part of this commitment to local young people we are able to provide three grants of up to a maximum of £50 or less, to groups/projects that meet the following criteria.

·        The funding will be used to benefit young people aged 8-20 in the South Cerney area.

·        The application is made by a young person/s or group leader.

·        A report is made following use of the funding that details how the money was used and how it was of benefit.
