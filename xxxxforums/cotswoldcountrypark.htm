This forum is so that users can air their views on the changes to the access at Cotswold Country Park (aka Keynes Country Park) brought in by the new leaseholders. Specifically it is to do with the fee levied to use the paths around the lakes. The Parish Council voted unanimously at its meeting to support Somerford Keynes and Ashton Keynes Parish Councils in their applications to add the current paths in the vicinity of the park to the Public Rights of Way (PROWs) of both Gloucestershire and Wiltshire. A similar exercise as that taken by South Cerney PC on the Barracks footpath. Although no commitment was given to financially support the action consideration would be given to such a request in the future.
