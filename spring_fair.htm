Spring Street Fair
2011

The Street Fair is an annual event which raises money for many local clubs and charities. Planning for the Street Fair is in full swing to make 2011 as fun as ever. Last year's event was very well attended and it was great to see so many families on fair day. The duck race was once again a particular favourite. Last year we were aware however that there were large numbers of people in Silver Street whilst traffic was trying to get through. We are conscious about safety of those attending the event, particularly at the time of the duck race. To improve safety we are proposing a road closure in Silver Street for 2011.

To be granted a road closure we need to apply to the District Council, but, before we do, we would like to receive any comments or suggestions from residents, particularly those affected in Silver Street, Church Lane, School Lane and Bow Wow. We intend to ask for closure between 9am - 5pm on the Monday only and to close the road between Timbrells Close and Meadow Way. Please see below for a plan. If you have any comments please email streetfair@southcerney.com or letters to The Church Office marked 'Street Fair, Street Closure', by 15th January 2011.

This is only a proposal at this stage and should we make an application residents are able to contact the District Council directly to make their opinions known.

Thank you
Street Fair Committee
