South Cerney Map

This map of the village was originally created by Les Tresadern on behalf of The South Cerney Trust who have granted us permission to reproduce electronically here.

To start, click on the miniature map to the right. This will give you the whole map rather small, but with the screen width.

There are two further levels of enlargement. Click your left mouse button anywhere on the maps to drop to the next level of scale, (apart from the bottom scale of course).  Where appropriate click close to the edge of a map, to move across to the next section, or in the corner to move diagonally.

To leave the maps or come up a level, select a new option from the main menu on the left.
