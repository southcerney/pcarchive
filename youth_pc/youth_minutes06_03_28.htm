Claire introduced meeting and gave out packs with information about how to develop a YPC. We agreed that this was our final public meeting and that during this meeting we needed to decide whether the group would self-elect or decide upon an election strategy.

 

Our objectives are to,

 

  • Develop a Youth Parish Council for South Cerney young people.
  • Enable young people to consult their peers and ascertain local needs.
  • Devise and run projects that enhance the resources available to local young people.
  • Raise the profile of young people and allow them to be decision makers in their community.
  • Enable the council to design their own structure, have appropriate committee’s and policy to ensure that they function as Youth Parish Council.
  • To directly liaise and work with the existing Parish Council.

 

 

The group requested that they were left alone to debate how they were to proceed with the formation of the council.

 

Feedback included,

 

  • The young people decided to put themselves forward to be the SCYPC.
  • They assigned roles & some responsibilities.
