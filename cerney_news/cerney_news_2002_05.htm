Cerney News Announcement
May 2002

WEB SITE

The South Cerney Parish Council web site is now up and running at www.southcerney.org.uk

The site has been created for the benefit of all who live in or are interested in the villages of South Cerney and Cerney Wick. It contains much useful information about the villages, both past and present, and it contains links to many other "local" sites.

There is a Comments page for you to send messages to the Clerk of the Council, who will pass on your remarks to the Chairman or the relevant committee as appropriate. It also contains Forums where everyone can have an opportunity to air their views, and to read other people's comments on various topics that affect the villages. The site will be continuously updated with the minutes of the monthly Parish Council meetings, and other relevant information, so please keep coming back to it.

ANNUAL PARISH MEETING

The Annual Parish Meeting will be held in the Village Hall on Wednesday 15 May at 7.30pm. This meeting presents an opportunity for any resident to raise matters of concern and put questions to members of the Parish Council, your local Cotswold District Councillors and County Councillor.
