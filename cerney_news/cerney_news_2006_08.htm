Cerney News Announcement
August 2006


Protect Your Own Property
Smartwater is a brand new crime prevention device to mark personal property and register it with a specialised company or the police. A Smartwater presentation will take place at 7.30pm on Tuesday 3rd October in the Village Hall. Residents are encouraged to come along to the demonstration where PC Jon Palfrey Rural Beat & Wildlife Crime Liaison Officer, David Bragg and Andy Fogden our Cotswold Neighbourhood Watch and Community Liaison Officers will attend. Further information on the scheme can be seen on the police website www.gloucestershire.police.uk

Calor Gas Village of the Year Awards
South Cerney has again starred in scooping three awards in the prestigious Calor Gas Village of the Year Award competition. Youth Parish Councillors Thomas Nicholas and Joe Lodge collected the £500 first prize for the Young People category. Another £500 first prize was presented to the Parish Council for the IT category and a runner up award in the Building Community Life category completed the achievement. Councillors Nicholas, Stuart and Squire represented the Parish Council at the event.

Thames Water Meeting - Rearranged Date
As Geoffrey Clifton-Brown MP is unable to attend the meeting to discuss the progress of Thames Water’s plan for the refurbishment of the sewerage system scheduled for October 6th it has been rearranged for Friday September 22nd at 2pm in the Village Hall when he will chair the meeting. All invited.
