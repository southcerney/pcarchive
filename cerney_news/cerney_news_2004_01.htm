Cerney News Announcement
January 2004

PUBLIC MEETING
Lt Col S. English, Commanding Officer 29 Regt Duke of Gloucester Barracks has agreed to attend a meeting of Parish Councillors prior to a Parish Council meeting on 11 February. The Parish Council would welcome members of the public to attend this meeting which will start at 7pm in the Committee Room of the Village Hall in School Lane. The meeting is scheduled to last 30 minutes starting with a short presentation by Lt Col English followed by a question and answer session from Parish Councillors and members of the public.

INCONSIDERATE PARKING
THROUGHOUT SOUTH CERNEY
Complaints are being received with increasing regularity about vehicles which are parked on pavements and inconsiderately throughout residential areas in the village. Besides it being a nuisance to residents of the streets affected there is a fear that such behaviour will hamper access to emergency vehicles. Motorists who park their vehicles anywhere are reminded to behave responsibly. It just might be that the fire engine or ambulance on a critical mission could be delayed because of where you have parked.
