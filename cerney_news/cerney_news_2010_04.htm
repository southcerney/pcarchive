Cerney News Announcement
APRIL 2010

ANNUAL PARISH MEETING

The Annual Parish Meeting will be held in the village hall on Wednesday 19th May at 7.30pm. This is your chance to hear reports from and ask questions of the main bodies and organisations in our village. Speakers will include representatives from the police, Army, churches, school, Parish Council, District Council and County Council.

ROBERT FRANKLIN WAY PLAY EQUIPMENT

After a recent inspection, Parish Councillors have come to the conclusion that the Train Unit on the enclosed children's play area at Robert Franklin Way play area must be replaced. The Parish Council would like to hear from residents with suggestions for its replacement The new play equipment must be suitable for younger children and all recommendations will be discussed. Please send your choice preferably to the clerk@southcerney.org.uk, by post to the address as promulgated on the notice board or inform your Parish Councillor with your favoured solution.

DOG MESS

Complaints continue to be received about the amount of dog litter which is being left in public places by the anti-social people who fail to clean up after their pets. If caught, those guilty should be aware that the Cotswold Community Wardens will be taking steps to prosecute them. The problem is more acute in the vicinity of Beverstone Road and Gypsy Lane area. The majority of pet owners do clean up after their dogs.

CONGRATULATIONS

Three residents were awarded two Runners Up prizes in a Tree Photo competition organised by the Tree Warden Group at Cotswold District Council. Congratulations to Maurice Taylor, Lisa and Ruby Dickson who were the successful entrants.
