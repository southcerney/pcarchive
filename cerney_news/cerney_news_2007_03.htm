Cerney News Announcement
March 2007


REPORTING VANDALISM

Could all members of the community please inform the police, a Parish Councillor or other authority if they witness any act of vandalism or unsocial behaviour. Useful telephone numbers to report any such incidents are as follows: Cirencester Police 0845 090 1234, Cotswold District Council 01285 623000, Gloucestershire Highways 0800 0 514 514 or the Parish Clerk 01285 644864. Parish Councillors telephone numbers are displayed on the notice board in Clarks Hay.

YOUR VOTE IS IMPORTANT

The local elections for Parish and District Councillors takes place on Thursday May 3rd. To ensure that democracy is seen to be working for you everyone eligible should exercise their right to vote. Don’t just ignore the event by thinking that your vote is not important. It is.

VILLAGE EVENT DIARY

Did you know that a Village Event Diary is published on the South Cerney Parish Council Web Site (www.southcerney.org.uk)? We want to increase the scope and use of this so if you wish to promote or inform people of forthcoming events in the village please complete the form on the web site events page or pass the information to Raymond Fenton. He can be contacted either by email at rfenton@southcerney.com or by telephoning 01285 860395.

MONEY, MONEY, MONEY

The Parish Council at the penultimate meeting of this session, voted unanimously in favour of awarding grants to local organisations if the proposed project is supported through its inclusion in the Village Plan.
