Cerney News Announcement
May 2005

Village Plan

The Parish Council are in the throes of preparing a village plan to take us through the next five years. Some of the areas of activity concern traffic, traffic speed, youth activities, cycle paths, activities for the elderly, etc.

There will be an Open Evening on Thursday 23rd June 2005 in the Village Hall from 7.00 pm until 9.00 pm. You are cordially invited to come and view the draft plan and add any ideas you may have. Come and talk to us and, if you wish join a working party to help us achieve an even better South Cerney and Cerney Wick.
