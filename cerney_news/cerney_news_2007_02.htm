Cerney News Announcement
February 2007


REPORTING VANDALISM

Once again vandals have been disfiguring our bus shelter, outside the Limes at the junction of Clarks Hay, High Street and Station Road, by spraying paint on its walls. This mindless activity is expensive to clean up and is a burden on every Council Tax payer in the community. If any one is aware who is to blame it is their civic duty to inform the police accordingly. Useful telephone numbers to report any such incidents are as follows:
Cirencester Police 0845 090 1234, Cotswold District Council 01285 623000,
Gloucestershire Highways 0800 0 514 514
or the Parish Clerk 01285 644864.

HIGH STREET CROSSING – GLOUCESTERSHIRE HIGHWAYS

The Parish Council has received a report from GCC stating that the case for the provision of a pedestrian crossing is not justified. This will become an agenda item at the next Council Meeting on Wednesday 14th March.

SEWERAGE WORKS

Confirmation that the upgrading of the sewerage system will not commence until 5th March at the earliest has been received from Thames Water. The work will be completed by mid-August.

CANDIDATES WANTED FOR OUR PARISH COUNCIL ELECTIONS IN MAY

So far four residents have shown an interest in becoming Parish Councillors and asked for nomination forms. These will be dispatched as soon as possible after receipt from the Cotswold District Council. Would any resident, 18 years old or above, who would like to serve as a Parish Councillor please contact the Electoral Office at Cotswold District Council 01285 623009 or Mr. M. McKee, Parish Clerk 01285 644864 for nomination forms?
