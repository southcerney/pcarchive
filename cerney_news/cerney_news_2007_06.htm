Cerney News Announcement
June 2007


BLEDISLOE CUP COMPETITION 2007
Judging of the "Best Kept Village in Gloucestershire" is taking place between 16th June and 1st July. Can we all please make a special effort to ensure that our village is as tidy as possible during the judging? If we are successful in the first round then the next inspection will be between 21st July and 5th August.

EMERGENCY TELEPHONE NUMBERS
The emergency telephone number to use if you are experiencing a gas supply problem is 0800 111 999; a water supply problem is 0845 9200 800 or email customer.feedback@thameswater.co.uk; an electrical problem 0845 7 70 80 90.

FLY TIPPING
We are having too many incidents of illegal fly tipping in the Parish. If you see such tipping taking place, record the vehicle number and telephone the police and then Roger Sollis at the Cotswold District Council. 623000.

FLY POSTING
Following the recent new Law the fixing of posters on road signs, lamp posts, etc is now illegal. Again the Cotswold District Council should be notified if you see this taking place.

SUSPICIOUS ACTIVITY AND ANTI-SOCIAL BEHAVIOUR
Anyone who is seen acting suspiciously should be reported to the Gloucestershire Constabulary on 0845 090 1234 asking for our PCSO Lesley Pooley
