WAR MEMORIAL

The Parish Council is now moving on to the next stage of the consultation on moving the War Memorial. To date Parish Councillors have met with Col Bennett of the War Memorials Trust who was in favour of the plan to move it. Mike Napper (CDC Planning) who had discussed the proposal with the Conservation & Design Officer and both had no adverse comments regarding the scheme, subject to the satisfaction of the Highways Officer. Mark Power, Gloucestershire County Council Area Manager (Cheltenham, Cotswold, Tewkesbury) Highways Development Management had no objection to the move. Parish Councillors agreed that a few individuals were not in favour but some of those had been convinced after seeing plans which are on display on the notice board in Clarks Hay. Generally Parish Councillors gauged the response from the community as positive to the plan.
