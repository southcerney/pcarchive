Cerney News Announcement
APRIL 2011

CAMAS PLANNING APPLICATION ALERT

Concerned residents should be aware that a revised outline planning application has been submitted by Pegasus Planning Group on behalf of Harrow Estates for the parcel of land behind the Limes to be known as the Mallards. Representations are encouraged to be sent to the Planning Authority at CDC with a copy to the Clerk of the Parish Council, Mr M. McKee, 6 North Home Road, Cirencester GL7 1DP or by email to clerk@southcerney.org.uk. The Parish Council has been assured that previous correspondence regarding the application will be kept on file at the District Council. The timescale allowed for responses is very short and every effort must be made by objectors and supporters to comply with the deadline of 20th April 2011. A public meeting will be held in the Village Hall, School Lane, South Cerney on Wednesday 6th April at 7.30 pm for members of the public to air their views on the proposals.

ELECTIONS AND REFERENDUM 2011

It has been confirmed that the UK Parliamentary Voting System Referendum will be held on the same day as our District and Parish Council Elections, Thursday 5th May 2011.

The Parish Council and District seats are subject to election -uncontested Parish Councils and District Wards will be known by the afternoon of Thursday 7th April at the very latest.

Nomination Packs for the Parish Council and District Council Wards are available from the District Council's offices at Trinity Road, Cirencester.

YOUTH LITTER PICK EXERCISE

The Parish Council wishes to congratulate all those young people who took part in the recent litter picking exercise. The Youth Group reported at a recent meeting of the Parish Council that 9 bags of litter had been collected. The community must be very grateful for the efforts shown.
