R-WAY COMMITTEE

This youth group, supported by the Parish Council, is now established in the village and is doing valuable volunteer work in the community. It has taken over from Mr Wrightson, who for many years kept the open ditch in Station Road free from rubbish. It is also involved in managing the compost material on the Upper Up playing fields. The group is currently advertising for a youth worker to coordinate its activities. It is also looking for more recruits to join in the fun and games. If you are 13 years old and up to 19 years you will be made very welcome. The group meets on Monday evenings at 6 o'clock mainly outside Londis and go from there to wherever for its activities. Contact Chris Wearing on mobile: 07581698353 (wickedwearing123@hotmail.co.uk) or Jasmin Tresadern (jasmin_tresadern@hotmail.com) for further information.

REGISTERING SEPTIC TANKS WITH ENVIRONMENT AGENCY

Please be aware that, to comply with recent legislation, howeowners with a septic tank or a package sewage treatment plant must register their systems with the Environment Agency as soon as possible, or obtain an environmental permit. The Environment Agency need to know where all the systems are, so they can focus their work on those contributing to environmental problems. More details, including the registration application process, can be found at www.environment-agency.gov.uk/homeandleisure/118753.aspx. A brief reminder will be issued on the Distraict Council's website www.cotswold.gov.uk. If you do not register, ignorance of the law is not a defence.
