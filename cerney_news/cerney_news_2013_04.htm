Cerney News Announcement
APRIL 2013

ANNUAL PARISH MEETING

The Annual Parish Meeting will be held in the United Church Hall in Clarks Hay at 7.30 pm on the evening of 15th May. All residents are encouraged to attend. Representatives of local organisations have been invited to give a short report on their activities and plans.

VILLAGE PLAN

Representatives from the Parish Council and Chris Cowcher from Gloucestershire Rural Community Council will be manning a stand at the Street Fair on Monday 27th May to seek suggestions for inclusion in the new Village Plan. If you have anything to add to the plan please come along and tell us.

EDDIE CURTIS

Eddie Curtis who has cleaned out the bus shelters, collected litter and generally kept the village in a very tidy condition on behalf of the Parish Council for many years has decided to retire. Eddie was a conscientious worker and will be missed. But, as they say, life moves on and now there is a vacancy to be filled. Would any resident interested in carrying on with the duties please contact the Clerk by either telephoning 01285 644864 or emailing clerk@southcerney.org.uk for further details.

WAR MEMORIAL

The plans for the resiting of the War Memorial at the junction of High Street and Broadway Lane will be available for inspection at the United Church Hall on Saturday 4th May from 10 am to midday. A reduced size version is on display in the glass fronted notice board in Clarks Hay opposite the Post Office.

COMMUNITY SPEED WATCH

Andrew West has volunteered to take over the organisation of the Speed Watch scheme aiming to reduce the numbers of speeding motorists in our parish. Andy needs more volunteers to join his team. For further details contact him by emailing sccsw@yahoo.co.uk or telephone and leave a message on 01285 898 609.
