Christmas Cards – unique to South Cerney

For Christmas 2006, All Hallows Church is selling Christmas cards and jigsaws in aid of the West Window Appeal. The high quality 6”x4” full gloss cards, priced at £3.70 for 10, feature a brightly coloured Christmas scene (the flight into Egypt) from the West Window. By purchasing these cards this year, you will be helping to restore an important feature of our historic church building.

 Jigsaws – an ideal Christmas gift with a local flavour

These beautifully produced and challenging wooden jigsaws feature a number of scenes from the West Window and come in two sizes – 40 pieces, £5 and 250 pieces, £22.50. They are suitable for adults and older children and are completely different from the normal cardboard jigsaws available in the shops.

Order and Sales Information

Green leaflets, which were sent out with the Cerney News have all the information. Further copies are available from All Hallows Church Office (in the United Church, next to the fish and chip shop) and at the back of All Hallows Church.

Closing date for card orders - 10th November

Closing date for jigsaw orders – 4th November

There are also plenty of opportunities for non-ordered sales. Dates are listed in the green leaflet and in the display in the Church Office window where sample cards and jigsaws can also be viewed.
